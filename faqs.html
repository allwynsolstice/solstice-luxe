FAQs

Your questions, answered

Why book with Solstice Luxe Travel instead of direct?
Through our global partnerships, we secure exclusive amenities — breakfast, resort credits, upgrades (when available), and VIP recognition — benefits not available when booking directly online.
Do you charge a planning or design fee?
Hotel‑only bookings come at no additional cost to you. For complex, tailor‑made itineraries, a transparent design fee may apply — always disclosed upfront.
Can you arrange flights and private transfers?
Yes. Whether first‑class, private jet, or commercial, we arrange air travel and smooth connections with chauffeured transfers.
Do you offer 24/7 support while I’m traveling?
Absolutely. Our concierge team is available around the clock to assist with changes, emergencies, and special requests.
Can you help with celebrations and special occasions?
Yes — honeymoons, anniversaries, destination weddings, and milestone trips receive dedicated planning and special touches.
Are your services only for luxury hotels?
We specialize in leading luxury properties and also curate boutique stays, private villas, and unique lodges.
Do you work with families and groups?
Definitely. We craft seamless itineraries for families with children, multigenerational trips, and curated group journeys.
Is travel insurance required?
We strongly recommend comprehensive coverage to protect your investment and peace of mind. We can connect you with trusted providers.
Can I book last‑minute trips with you?
Yes — while advance planning ensures the best options, we often arrange last‑minute getaways with excellent results.
What makes Solstice Luxe different?
We go beyond logistics to create experiences that reflect your personal style and pace — elevating travel into something effortless and unforgettable.
